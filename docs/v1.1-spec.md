# SportsCheat v1.1 — "The Curious Outsider" Update

## Design Principle
Treat the non-fan as a curious outsider who wants to belong, not as someone who needs to be converted into a fan. Reduce the social cost of not knowing.

## Features

### 1. Story-First Framing (Olympics-style)
- Lead every blurb with the HUMAN narrative: underdog, rivalry backstory, athlete personal arc
- Format: Who is this person? What did they sacrifice? What are the stakes?
- Drive to Survive template, not SportsCenter template

### 2. "Why Should I Care?" Context Cards
- Before any matchup: one paragraph on why this matters culturally, historically, or socially
- Not a stats dump — assume intelligence, fill in context gaps

### 3. Social/Cultural Layer
- Treat cultural events (halftime shows, commercials, celebrity appearances) as first-class
- Separate section for "The Scene" alongside game coverage
- Super Bowl: 60%+ watch for non-game reasons — serve that audience

### 4. Athlete-First Browsing
- Follow athletes as cultural figures, not just roster entries
- 62% of Gen Z identify as fans of an athlete rather than a sport
- Surface: LeBron, Serena, Biles, Mahomes as entry points

### 5. Adjustable Depth / Plain Language Mode
- Toggle between "insider" and "plain language" modes
- Replace jargon with plain descriptions (e.g., "pick-six" → "the defender caught the ball and scored")
- Never assume prior knowledge by default

### 6. Women's Sports as Equal Entry Point
- WNBA, NWSL, WTA as first-class sports alongside Big 4
- WNBA gained 17M new fans in 2024; 53% started in last 3 years
- More inclusive community norms = what non-fans want

### 7. Social Coordination Tools
- "Watching with others" features
- Conversation starters for non-fans in a fan crowd
- Mid-game explainers you can pull up live
- Simple shared commentary

### 8. Spoiler-Free by Default
- No scores shown until user taps to reveal
- Non-fans watch for the experience, not the outcome
- Protect that by default

## Technical Changes
- New types: `AthleteSpotlight`, `CulturalEvent`, `WhyShouldICare`
- Extended `Sport` type: add `wnba`, `nwsl`, `wta`
- New prompts: story-first digest, cultural layer, plain language mode
- New UI components: SpoilerGuard, JargonToggle, CulturalCard, AthleteCard
- Settings state: `plainLanguage`, `spoilerFree`, `selectedSports`
